== Description ==
### Awesome Tube - Video Stream & Blog WordPress Plugin

Awesome Tube is a WordPress plugin that prioritizes video as a medium for information and also as a learning medium. It is suitable for those of you who want to share information or learn in your community.

Awesome Tube makes your content more exclusive. With Awesome Tube, you can create a video collection website for you, your business, or your community.

### What You Will Get?
By using an Awesome Tube, you don't need any coding skills. With only a few steps of creation, your video content is ready to show on Your WordPress site. Awesome Tube provides a beautiful video page template.
